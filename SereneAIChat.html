Mental Health Statistics Among Students in Malaysia

Recent data indicates a significant rise in mental health issues among students in Malaysia. According to the National Health Morbidity Survey (NHMS), the prevalence of mental health problems in this group increased from 7.9% (approximately 424,000 individuals) in 2019 to 16.5% (around 922,318 individuals) in 2023.

For more detailed information, visit Selangor Journal.
